Nuclear Commando

Retro assault. Tactical objectives. No room for hesitation.

A rogue military network called the Ashen Guard has activated hidden enrichment and missile production sites. Strike hard, disable their war machine, and extract before the countdown expires.

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Jump: Up Arrow (hold to jump higher)
  • Crouch: Down Arrow
  • Roll: R (or Down + Move + Shoot)
  • Shoot: Space
  • Cycle Weapons: A / B
  • Pause: P
  • Fullscreen: F
