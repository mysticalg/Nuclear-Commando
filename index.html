Nuclear Commando

Retro assault. Tactical objectives. No room for hesitation.

A rogue military network called the Ashen Guard has activated hidden enrichment and missile production sites. Strike hard, disable their war machine, and extract before the countdown expires.

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Aim: Up Arrow or Up + Direction
  • Crouch: Down Arrow
  • Jump: Space (hold to jump higher)
  • Drop Through Catwalks: Down + Space
  • Climb Grates/Walls: Hold Up or Down while touching them
  • Hang Bars: Jump, Up, or Fire into overhead bars
  • Drop From Bars: Hold Down
  • Roll: R
  • Shoot: Z
  • Aim Lock: Hold X
  • Low Aim: Hold X + Down + Left/Right
  • Cycle Weapons: A / B
  • Mute: M
  • Pause: P
  • Fullscreen: F
